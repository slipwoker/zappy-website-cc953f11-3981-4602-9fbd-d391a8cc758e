About Poligem

A diamond jewellery manufacturer dedicated to precision craftsmanship, modern design, and the pursuit of perfect light performance in every cut.

Our Point Of View

We believe diamonds are light sculptures—engineered facets that transform raw brilliance into deliberate beauty. Our approach rejects excess in favor of restraint, where every angle, every proportion, and every polish mark serves a singular purpose: to capture and release light with maximum clarity.

From square emerald cuts to precision fancy shapes, we craft each piece with the discipline of a watchmaker and the vision of a modernist architect. This is jewelry stripped to its essence—no ornament for ornament's sake, only geometry, light, and intention.

What We Protect

Transparency

Every diamond jewellery piece comes with full certification and origin documentation. We walk you through cut grades, clarity maps, and carat specifications so you understand exactly what you're choosing—no hidden details, no marketing fluff.

Craft

Our workshop combines precision CAD modeling with hand-finishing by master setters. Each prong, bezel, and pavé row is inspected under 10× magnification. We don't rush production—timelines reflect the meticulous work required for heirloom-quality results.

Calm Luxury

Poligem rejects the high-pressure sales environment. Our consultations are private, unhurried, and focused on education. You'll never feel pushed toward a decision—luxury should feel considered, confident, and entirely yours.

Measured choices • Clear guidance • Timeless finishing

From Selection To Finish

Select

Every diamond journey begins with precision. Our gemologists evaluate each rough stone for its unique characteristics—clarity, color, and natural inclusions—ensuring only exceptional candidates enter our atelier. We source ethically, prioritizing traceability and brilliance in every carat.

Refine

Master craftsmen employ laser-guided cutting technology alongside traditional hand-finishing techniques. Each facet is calculated to maximize light return, creating our signature square and fancy cuts. This meticulous process transforms raw potential into architectural perfection, where geometry meets artistry.

Reveal

The final stage unveils the diamond's true character. Through careful polishing and quality verification, we achieve museum-grade finish. Each piece undergoes rigorous certification before being set into bespoke jewelry designs. The result: a light source that captures attention and holds it effortlessly.
