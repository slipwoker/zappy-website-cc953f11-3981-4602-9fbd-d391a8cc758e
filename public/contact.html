Quick Answers

How do I choose the right diamond cut?

Consider your personal style and the jewelry setting. Round brilliants offer maximum sparkle, while square princess and emerald cuts deliver modern geometry. Fancy cuts like cushion and oval add unique character. Our specialists guide you through cut quality, proportions, and how each shape interacts with light.

What should I know about diamond clarity?

Clarity grades range from Flawless (FL) to Included (I). Most inclusions are microscopic and invisible to the naked eye. We recommend VS2 or higher for optimal value—these diamonds appear flawless without the premium of IF grades. Each stone is certified with detailed clarity mapping.

What's the typical timeline for custom pieces?

Custom jewelry typically takes 4-6 weeks from design approval to completion. This includes CAD rendering review, stone selection, casting, setting, and final polishing. Rush services are available for special occasions. We provide progress updates at each milestone so you're never left wondering.

How do I care for my diamond jewelry?

Clean your diamonds monthly with warm water and mild soap, using a soft brush to remove buildup. Avoid harsh chemicals and remove jewelry during physical activities. Store pieces separately to prevent scratching. We offer complimentary professional cleaning and inspection annually to maintain brilliance.

What information should I bring to a consultation?

Bring inspiration images, budget range, and any existing jewelry you'd like to complement or redesign. If you have specific stone preferences (carat, cut, color), share those details. No technical knowledge required—our diamond company specialists translate your vision into precise specifications and guide every decision.

Can I select diamonds remotely?

Absolutely. We provide high-resolution imagery, 360° videos, and detailed certification documents for remote selection. Video consultations let you view stones under different lighting conditions. We ship fully insured with tracking, and offer a 14-day return window if the piece doesn't meet your expectations.
