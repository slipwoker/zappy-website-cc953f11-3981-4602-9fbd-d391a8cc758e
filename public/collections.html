How To Read The Light.

Cut

The cut determines how light travels through the stone—ideal proportions return brilliance directly to your eye. A well-executed cut transforms raw potential into focused sparkle. We grade on a scale where58facets meet at mathematically precise angles, ensuring maximum light return and that signature fire across the table.

Clarity

Clarity measures internal characteristics—nature's fingerprints. Most inclusions remain invisible to the naked eye; what matters is their position and size relative to the stone's critical light paths. A grade ofVS1means inclusions exist under magnification but won't compromise the visual experience or structural integrity in daily wear.

Carat

Carat weight tells only part of the story—spread and presence matter equally. A1.00ctround brilliant measures approximately 6.5mm across the table, but cut quality determines whether that weight translates to visual impact. We prioritize how a diamond carries its carat weight, not just the number on the certificate.
